About

Better coffee gear decisions, without the noise.

The Crema Report is an independent resource for people who take home coffee seriously — the folks weighing up their first real espresso machine, hunting for a grinder that actually holds a setting, or quietly building a setup one upgrade at a time.

Buying coffee gear should be exciting. Too often it's just confusing: marketing claims, inflated pressure numbers, sponsored "top 10" lists, and a hundred near-identical products. We exist to cut through that.

What we do

We publish clear, in-depth buying guides for home coffee and espresso equipment — espresso machines, grinders, kettles, scales, tampers, milk frothers, and the rest of the rabbit hole. Every guide is built to answer one real question: which one should you actually buy, and why?

How our guides are researched

We're transparent about our method, because trust matters more than hype:

  • Manufacturer specifications and documented features
  • Published expert consensus from the coffee community
  • Aggregated, verified customer reviews — the patterns across hundreds of real owners

We do not stage fake hands-on tests or invent reviewer personas. When we recommend something, it's because the research points there — not because a brand paid for placement.

How we stay free. The Crema Report is reader-supported. Some links on this site are affiliate links — if you buy through them, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. It never changes which products we recommend or what we say about them.

Who it's for

Beginners who want to get it right the first time, and enthusiasts who enjoy the upgrade culture as much as the coffee. If you'd rather spend twenty minutes reading than three hundred dollars guessing — this is for you.
